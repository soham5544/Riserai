Privacy Policy for Riser.AI

Effective Date: August 20, 2025

App Version: 0.0.1

Developer: Soham Sardar (RYZ.X)

Introduction

Riser.AI is an AI-powered personal assistant app developed by under the RYZ.X initiative. The app is designed to provide voice interaction, answer questions, open apps, set reminders, and assist users with daily activities. Your privacy is very important to us, and we are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

  • Voice Input / Queries: The app processes your voice/text input to generate responses.
  • App Permissions: The app may request permissions such as microphone access to function properly.
  • No Personal Registration Required: You don’t need to create an account to use Riser.AI.

How We Use Information

  • To provide AI-based responses and app functionality.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.
  • To open apps and perform actions requested by the user.

Data Sharing & Security

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.
  • User queries are processed only to generate answers and are not permanently stored.
  • We use industry-standard methods to keep your information safe.

Permissions Used

  • Microphone: For voice input.
  • Internet: To fetch information and provide responses.
  • App Access: To open supported apps like Google, YouTube, Instagram, etc.

Children’s Privacy

Riser.AI is safe for general use but not specifically designed for children under 13. Parental guidance is recommended.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with a new “Effective Date.”
